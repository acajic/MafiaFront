Sheriff plays with the Citizens.
Sheriff has a special ability to reveal exact roles of all residents that died during the time this user was sheriff. After this ability is utilized, on the next morning, the true roles of some deceased residents will be revealed to the entire city.
By default, Sheriff can only use his special ability once in a single game.
When a sheriff dies, a random ordinary Citizen is selected to become the new sheriff. If there are no more ordinary citizens alive when the sheriff dies, nobody becomes the new sherff.
