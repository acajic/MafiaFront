Terrorist plays on board with the Mafia.
A Terrorist can see other mafia members and participate in mafia voting like an ordinary mafia member.
They have additional option to perform a terrorist bombing attack. For this action, they target a single resident as a victim. During the bombing attack they kill that victim, themselves and a number of random collateral victims in the process. The default number of collateral victims is 1.
Terrorist bombing action is an asynchronous action. That means that it can be executed in the middle of the day, or in the middle of the night.
