Deputy plays with the Citizens.
Deputy has a special ability to reveal exact roles of all residents that died during the time this user was operating as a deputy. When this ability is utilized, a Deputy will see the result of this action on the next morning. The true roles of some deceased residents will be revealed to his eyes only.
By default, Deputy can only use his special ability once in a single game.
When a deputy dies, a random ordinary Citizen is selected to become the new deputy. If there are no more ordinary citizens alive when a deputy dies, nobody becomes the new deputy and the games continues without one.
