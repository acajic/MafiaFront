Journalist, also known as Weak Detective, plays on board with the Citizens.
He has a special ability to investigate a single resident per night.
As a result, on the next morning, a journalist will be informed that the investigated resident is a mafia member only if:
  1. that resident is, in fact, a mafia member;
  2. that resident took part in mafia voting on the night he was investigated.

Both of the stated conditions must be met in order for a journalist to receive an affirmative response. Otherwise, a journalist will be informed that the investigated resident is not a mafia member.
