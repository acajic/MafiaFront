Silent Sheriff plays with the Citizens.
Silent Sheriff has a special ability to reveal exact roles of all residents that died during the time this user was sheriff. When this ability is utilized, a Silent Sheriff will see the result of this action on the next morning. The true roles of some deceased residents will be revealed to his eyes only.
By default, Silent Sheriff can only use his special ability once in a single game.
When a silent sheriff dies, a random ordinary Citizen is selected to become the new silent sheriff. If there are no more ordinary citizens alive when the silent sheriff dies, nobody becomes the new silent sherff.
